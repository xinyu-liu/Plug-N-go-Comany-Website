Made in the USA

Ask most Americans if they prefer products made by American workers, and they readily say, "Of course!" PlugnGo E-bikes stand out from others because we are the only electric bike company focused on making 'Made in USA' cool again. Affordability, style, servicing, quality, simple and functional E-bikes for the majority of Americans who don't want to spend a lot to go far and have fun while doing it is what were all about.

PlugnGo is creating jobs, keeping money circulating in our own country, employing Americans and decreasing our dependency on fossil fuels while contributing the health of our children and improving the environment. Our hope is to encourage more consumers to buy "Made in America," as well as influence American companies to keep their manufacturing in the U.S.A. Please join the PlugnGo Movement!
